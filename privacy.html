Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal identification information (Name, Email, Shipping Address) provided directly by you when you create an account, purchase a product, or contact us. We also collect specific non-personal data related to your pet (Dog's Name, State of Licensure, and uploaded Photo).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To fulfill and ship your physical diploma and personalized certification letter.
  • To operate the "National Database of Certified Holistic Therapy Dogs" (using your pet's name, photo, and license status).
  • To communicate with you regarding your order or account status.
  • To improve the efficiency and operation of the Services.

3. Data Security and Storage

We are committed to protecting your data. All sensitive authentication data is handled securely:

  • Passwords: User passwords are never stored in plain text. They are stored using a secure, industry-standard hashing algorithm (SHA-256) combined with a unique, user-specific salt.
  • Payment Data: We do not store or process your sensitive payment information (credit card numbers). All transactions are processed externally through PayPal, which adheres to its own stringent security standards.

4. Public Disclosure and Your Pet's Data (UGC)

By purchasing a license and uploading your dog's photo, you grant the Association a perpetual license to use, display, and distribute your pet's name and photo publicly on the Site (including the "Verify a License" page and the "Meet Our Dogs" page) as part of the public registry.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the address provided on our Contact Us page.
