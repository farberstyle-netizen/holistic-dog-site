Advocacy & Legislation

The HTDA tracks key legislative changes to promote the health, safety, and rights of companion animals across the country.

S.B. 104 - Veterinary Abuse Reporting Act (2025)

Effective: September 1, 2025

Summary: Mandates the creation of a centralized, confidential database for veterinary clinics to report suspected cases of animal abuse. Provides legal immunity for veterinarians who report such cases in good faith.

H.R. 72 - Safe Pet Transport Law (2024)

Effective: January 1, 2024

Summary: Establishes federal minimum standards for commercial animal transport, including climate controls and mandatory hydration breaks, effectively banning dangerous mass-transport practices.

A.B. 31 - Pet Wellness Fund Act (2023)

Effective: July 15, 2023

Summary: Creates a public fund subsidized by licensing fees to provide spay/neuter services and emergency veterinary care for low-income pet owners.
