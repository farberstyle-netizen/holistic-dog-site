    Fighting for Those without a Voice

    Federal Legislation (2024-2025)

    PAW Act of 2025

    H.R. 1842 — 119th Congress

    The People and Animals Well-being Act would amend the Internal Revenue Code to allow veterinary expenses for pets and service animals to be treated as medical care expenses for health savings accounts and flexible savings accounts.

    Goldie's Act

    H.R. 349 — 119th Congress

    This bill would amend the Animal Welfare Act to increase enforcement with respect to violations, strengthening protections for animals in commercial breeding facilities and research settings.

    Puppy Protection Act of 2023

    H.R. 1624 — 118th Congress

    Bipartisan legislation to amend the Animal Welfare Act providing for the humane treatment of dogs in commercial breeding operations, establishing minimum care standards and veterinary oversight requirements.

    Keeping Pets and Families Together Act

    H.R. 3709 — 118th Congress

    Would amend the Animal Welfare Act to facilitate cooperative agreements between the federal government and animal shelters, and award grants to support microchipping of dogs and cats in shelters.

    State Legislation (2024-2025)

    Louisiana Support and Service Animal Integrity Act

    Louisiana — Enacted 2024

    Restores integrity into the process of determining whether an individual has a disability requiring a support animal, establishing clear guidelines and preventing fraudulent misrepresentation.

    Iowa Assistance Animal Accommodations Act

    Iowa SF 2268 — Enacted 2024

    Comprehensive legislation relating to assistance animals and service animals, including reasonable accommodations for housing and requirements for findings of disabilities under state law.

    Massachusetts Animal Welfare Act

    Massachusetts S.B. 2908 — Signed January 2025

    Prevents the sale of animals under 8 weeks of age and prohibits roadside sale of animals, protecting both puppies and consumers from unethical practices.

    Colorado & Michigan Cage-Free Egg Laws

    Colorado & Michigan — Effective January 2025

    Laws banning the production and sale of eggs from caged hens came into force, protecting an estimated 10 million birds annually and advancing humane farming standards.

    Indiana Retail Dog Sale Regulations

    Indiana H.B. 1412 — Effective July 2024

    Regulates the retail sale of dogs, requiring registration with the Board of Animal Health, establishing mandatory disclosures and consumer warranties, and creating an inspection program for commercial breeders.

    Washington Humane Cosmetics Act

    Washington State — Effective January 2025

    Prohibits the sale of cosmetics that have been newly tested on animals, becoming the 12th state to pass such legislation and advancing cruelty-free industry standards.
