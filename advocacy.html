    Understanding Your Rights

    While Holistic Therapy Dogs do not grant the same public access rights as Service Animals under the ADA, they are vital components of mental health strategies. We advocate for the expansion of these rights based on merit and behavior.

    Fair Housing Act (FHA)

    Under the FHA, individuals with disabilities may request reasonable accommodations for assistance animals, including emotional support animals, in housing where pets are otherwise prohibited.

    Air Carrier Access Act (ACAA)

    Recent changes to the ACAA have narrowed definitions for air travel, recognizing Psychiatric Service Animals (PSAs) but often excluding ESAs. We are lobbying for a "Good Citizen" exception for therapy dogs with proven flight logs.
