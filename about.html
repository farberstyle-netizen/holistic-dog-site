About the Association

The Holistic Therapy Dog Association (HTDA) was founded on the principle that the most profound healing often comes not from a prescription, but from the intuitive, non-verbal bond shared between a human and an animal.

We are a commemorative body, not a legal one. We do not certify service animals. Instead, we provide a prestigious, formal honor for those exceptional dogs whose mere presence provides comfort, calms anxiety, and enriches the holistic well-being of their companions.

Board of Trustees

Dr. Bernard Gräber

Chairman of the Board of Trustees

A distinguished Swiss-Canadian equine veterinarian, Dr. Gräber has spent decades studying the intuitive, non-verbal communication between animals and humans. His extensive work with performance horses has provided unique insights into the nature of the holistic, spiritual bond. He champions the core mission of honoring the animal's role as a healer and ensures the Association maintains its prestigious standards and integrity.
