    About the Holistic Therapy Dog Association

    Celebrating the healing bond between humans and dogs since 2025

    Our Mission

    The Holistic Therapy Dog Association exists to honor and celebrate the profound therapeutic impact that dogs have on human well-being. We provide official recognition for dogs who bring comfort, joy, and healing to their human companions through our commemorative certification program.

    While our certifications are commemorative in nature and do not confer legal service animal status, they serve as a meaningful tribute to the special dogs who make our lives better every day.

    Important Disclaimer

    The Holistic Therapy Dog Association provides commemorative certifications that celebrate the therapeutic value of dogs. Our certifications are not legally recognized service animal registrations and do not confer any legal rights or access privileges under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) or any other legislation. They are intended as meaningful keepsakes to honor your dog's positive impact on your life.
