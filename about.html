About the Association

Founded on the principle that the most profound healing comes from the intuitive bond shared between human and animal.

Our Mission

"While traditional certifications focus on tasks and training, our singular mission is to provide the most formal, prestigious honor for the dogs whose very presence can heal the deepest, darkest wounds. We are the premier organization dedicated to commemorating these outstanding companions."

Leadership Team

Christian Farber

President

As President, Christian provides the core vision for the Association. He has long been an advocate for supporting those in need, championing the power of holistic and emotional well-being to build stronger communities. He is dedicated to building a global registry that celebrates these outstanding animals and honors their unique ability to heal.

Dr. Bernard Gräber

Chairman of the Board of Trustees

A distinguished Swiss-Canadian equine veterinarian, Dr. Gräber has spent decades studying the intuitive, non-verbal communication between animals and humans. He champions the core mission of honoring the animal's role as a healer and ensures the Association maintains its prestigious standards.
