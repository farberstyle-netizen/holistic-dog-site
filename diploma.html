The Official Diploma & Holder

A Prestigious Honor

Your certification is commemorated with a formal, 8.5" x 11" diploma. Each document is personalized with your dog's official registered name, the date of certification, and the signatures of the Association President and the Chairman of the Board of Trustees.

Printed on archival-quality, heavyweight parchment, the diploma features the Association's official seal, embossed in brilliant gold foil.

[ Photo of Diploma Holder ]

The Professional Holder

To protect and display your diploma, every order includes a professional-grade, padded leatherette holder.

Finished in a classic navy blue, the cover is adorned with a gold-embossed recreation of the Holistic Therapy Dog Association seal, making it a distinguished addition to any desk or bookshelf.
