    How It Works

    A simple, respectful process to recognize your dog's natural abilities.

    1. The Assessment

    Take our self-certifying quiz. We don't ask for tricks or obedience commands. We ask about temperament, connection, and the joy your dog brings to others.

    2. Registration

    Create your account and register your dog in the National Database. This creates a permanent, searchable record of their status.

    3. Recognition

    Receive your official embossed diploma and ID details. Your dog is now formally recognized as a Holistic Therapy Dog.
