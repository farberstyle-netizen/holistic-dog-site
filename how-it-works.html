    A Simple 4-Step Process

    Follow these steps to get your companion officially recognized in our registry.

    01.

    Create Your Account

    Before purchasing, you must create a secure account. This is where you will manage your license, update your shipping address, and view your order history.

    02.

    Submit Your Dog's Info

    Enter your dog's name exactly as you wish it to appear on the diploma. You will also upload one (1) clear photo for the public registry.

    03.

    Confirm & Pay

    During checkout, you will be shown a final preview of the name for the diploma. Please confirm the exact spelling. You will then be redirected to Stripe for secure payment.

    04.

    Receive Your Diploma & Join the Registry

    Your official diploma is shipped, and your dog's license is now active in our National Database of Certified Holistic Therapy Dogs for one year.
