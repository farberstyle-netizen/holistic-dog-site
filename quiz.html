The Holistic Therapy Dog Quiz

Does your dog have what it takes?

This 3-question self-certification is the first step to joining the elite ranks. Answer honestly to affirm your dog's unique spirit.

Question 1: Intuitive Bond

Does your dog often seem to sense your feelings or provide comfort without being asked, demonstrating an intuitive, spiritual, or heartfelt connection?

Question 2: Healing Presence

Do you consider your dog's presence to be a healing, compassionate, or calming force, enriching the lives of those they encounter?

Question 3: Formal Honor

Do you believe this profound, commemorative bond deserves to be formally recognized with the Association's highest honor?
