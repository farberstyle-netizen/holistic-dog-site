    Privacy Policy

    Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect personal information ("Personal Data") you provide when you register, purchase a license, or contact us. This includes:

    • Name
    • Email Address
    • Shipping Address
    • Pet Information (Dog's name, photo)

    2. How We Use Your Data

    We use your Personal Data to:

    • Fulfill your order and ship your diploma.
    • Manage your account and license status.
    • Publicly display your pet's name, photo, and license status as part of the public registry.

    3. Payment Processing

    We do not store or process your sensitive payment information (credit card numbers). All transactions are processed externally through Stripe, which adheres to its own stringent security standards.

    4. Public Disclosure and Your Pet's Data (UGC)

    By purchasing a license and uploading your dog's photo, you grant the Association a perpetual license to use, display, and distribute your pet's name and photo publicly on the Site (including the "Verify a License" page and the "Gallery" page) as part of the public registry.

    5. Contact Us

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact page.
