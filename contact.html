    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does certification processing take?
    Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Diplomas are shipped via USPS and arrive within 5-7 business days.
    Can I certify multiple dogs?
    Yes! You can certify as many dogs as you'd like. Each dog requires a separate certification and will receive their own unique diploma and database entry.
    Is this a legal service animal certification?
    No. Our certifications are commemorative in nature and do not confer legal service animal status under the ADA. They are meaningful keepsakes to honor your therapy dog.
    What if I need to update my shipping address?
    Contact us immediately using the form above or email support@holistictherapydogassociation.com with your order number. We can update addresses if the order hasn't shipped yet.
    Do certifications expire?
    Yes, certifications are valid for 1 year from the date of issue. You'll receive a reminder email when it's time to renew.
