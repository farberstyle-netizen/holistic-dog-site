Recognizing the Holistic Therapy Dog

A Title of Honor for an Outstanding Companion

A Holistic Therapy Dog is a companion whose intuitive presence and unconditional bond enriches the lives they touch on a spiritual level.

This certification bestows no privileges, rights, or access. It is about Honoring the profound, emotional connection that your dog builds with the souls around them. It is a commemorative title, the very highest issued by our Association, that celebrates their unique abilities and the profound impact they have in our lives.
